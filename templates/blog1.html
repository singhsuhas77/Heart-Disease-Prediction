Understanding Heart Diseases

Heart disease is a leading cause of death worldwide. It refers to various types of heart conditions, the most common being coronary artery disease (CAD), which can lead to heart attacks.

Common Types of Heart Diseases

Heart diseases include conditions like:

  • • Coronary Artery Disease (CAD)
  • • Heart Arrhythmias
  • • Heart Failure
  • • Heart Valve Disease
  • • Congenital Heart Defects

Symptoms of Heart Disease

Common symptoms of heart disease include:

  • • Chest pain or discomfort
  • • Shortness of breath
  • • Fatigue
  • • Dizziness or fainting
  • • Swelling in the legs, feet, or abdomen

Prevention Tips

To prevent heart disease, follow these guidelines:

  • • Maintain a healthy diet
  • • Exercise regularly
  • • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • • Manage stress levels
  • • Get regular health check-ups
